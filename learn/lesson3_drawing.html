Lesson 3: Draw Shapes

Let’s draw a square with Python!

from js import document ctx = document.getElementById("canvas").getContext("2d") side = 100 ctx.beginPath() ctx.moveTo(50, 50) for _ in range(4): ctx.lineTo(50 + side, 50) ctx.translate(side, 0) ctx.rotate(3.14159/2) ctx.stroke()
